FAQ

How do I pay?

You will pay once the site is completed and ready to be published. Payments can be made via bank transfer or PayPal.

Can I edit my site after it's live?

Yes - because the site is custom-coded, I offer maintenance plans if you'd rather I handle any updates or changes for you.

What if I don't have any content or images?

No worries. I can help create content, source high-quality images, or guide you on what to provide. We’ll make sure the site looks polished either way.

Will my site work on phones and tablets?

Absolutely. Every site I build is fully responsive, meaning it adapts to all screen sizes - phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.

Do you register the domain in my name?

Yes - the domain is yours. I register it through my system to make setup and management easier, but I can transfer it to you at any time if requested.

Can you host my existing website?

In most cases, yes. If your site is built in standard HTML/CSS/JS or WordPress, I can host it - though I’ll need to review the files first to confirm compatibility.

What happens if I stop paying for hosting?

You'll get a 30 days grace period or an agreement may be made in certain situations. After the grace period, the site goes offline, but I’ll keep a backup for 90 days in case you want to resume or transfer it elsewhere.
